About Us

Have you ever built a game at a hackathon or game jam—something with a message that mattered—only to watch it fade once the event ended? We know that feeling. Too many socially conscious projects disappear after their first moment in the spotlight.

This platform is a home for games that do more than entertain: games that teach, raise awareness, and spark conversation. Here, short-lived creations find a lasting stage, reaching new players, communities, and organizations that care about change.
